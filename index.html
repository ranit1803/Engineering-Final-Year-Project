About BioMedX Website

BioMedX is a smart health monitoring platform designed to help individuals take charge of their physical well-being with ease and clarity. Whether you're a student, professional, elderly individual, or someone simply interested in staying healthy, BioMedX combines technology, data, and AI to support your health journey in real time.

The homepage of BioMedX features carefully selected YouTube videos focused on health, fitness, lifestyle, and diet. These videos are meant to constantly remind and encourage users to adopt healthier habits in their daily lives. It serves as a gentle push toward consistent wellness through engaging and relatable content.

Once logged in, users can access their personal dashboard, where live health data from their smart surveillance jacket is displayed. This includes critical body readings such as heart rate, temperature, oxygen saturation, and more all presented through interactive graphs that make it easy to track changes and spot patterns over time.

A major highlight of BioMedX is the built-in AI-Doc, which uses advanced AI (powered by GPT-3.5 Turbo) to analyze user health data. It generates detailed reports, identifies abnormalities, and offers personalized suggestions to help users better understand their condition and take informed action.

Additionally, BioMedX includes a live location tracking feature through integrated maps, enabling users or healthcare professionals to monitor movement or pinpoint current location especially useful in case of emergencies or health alerts.
